Noisy LLMs and hallucination detection

May 2026

We've been building a unified framework to compare hallucination detection methods side by side: semantic entropy, perplexity, max-token probability, calibrated entropy, sink rate, and several white-box approaches.

What we've learned so far

  • No single method wins everywhere — dataset and model matter a lot.
  • White-box signals are informative but expensive; good ensembles often beat any one approach.
  • Annotation quality is the hidden variable in every benchmark.

Next steps

Run the full battery on TriviaQA and CoQA with smaller open models (Qwen3-0.6B, Llama-2-7B) and publish reproducible configs.

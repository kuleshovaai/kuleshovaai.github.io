Sink rate as an uncertainty signal

April 2026

Sink rate measures how quickly attention concentrates on sink tokens during generation. The hypothesis: when sinks activate strongly early, the model may be relying on shallow pattern-matching rather than careful reasoning.

Preliminary results

On TriviaQA, sink rate alone achieves modest AUC for hallucination detection — not competitive with semantic entropy, but cheap to compute and complementary in an ensemble.

Mechanistic angle

We're correlating sink dynamics with induction head activity. Early results suggest the signal is partly length-dependent, which needs normalization before it's useful in production.
